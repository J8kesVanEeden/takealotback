# CGSO Advisory Note 1 — Onus of proof under CPA s 56

## Proposition cited on the site

Within the 6-month CPA s 56 window, the practical burden sits with the supplier to rebut non-conformity. Bare assertion by the supplier ("it's wear and tear") is not evidence; the supplier must produce the technical assessment supporting the rejection.
