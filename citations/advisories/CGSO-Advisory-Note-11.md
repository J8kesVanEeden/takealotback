# CGSO Advisory Note 11 — Supply of Voetstoots Goods

## Proposition cited on the site

A voetstoots ("as-is") clause cannot disclaim the CPA's quality warranty. Where the buyer is a CPA consumer, any contractual voetstoots language is void to the extent it would waive s 55/s 56 protections.

Aligned with the holdings in *Vonk v Willow Crest Motors* (NCT) and *Fourie v Agenbag Motor Group* (NW Consumer Affairs Court).
