# Television Radio Centre v Sony Kabushiki Kaisha
## 1987 (2) SA 994 (A) | Appellate Division

## Proposition cited on the site

Where goods are **materially altered** after market, statutory exhaustion under TMA s 34(2)(d) does not apply. Parallel-imported goods with changed specification fall outside the exhaustion doctrine and may still infringe the mark.

## Citation note

The citation convention in SA legal practice varies — some commentary reverses the parties (Sony was the appellant). 1987 (2) SA 994 (A) is the controlling citation.
