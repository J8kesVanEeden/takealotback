# Frank & Hirsch (Pty) Ltd v A Roopanand Brothers (Pty) Ltd
## 1993 (4) SA 279 (A) | Appellate Division

## Proposition cited on the site

Where a separate SA copyright owner controls the **packaging / get-up** of goods, parallel imports can still be blocked on copyright grounds even where the trade-mark exhaustion defence under TMA s 34(2)(d) would otherwise apply. Copyright in packaging operates independently of trade-mark exhaustion.
