# Holmdene Brickworks (Pty) Ltd v Roberts Construction Co Ltd
## 1977 (3) SA 670 (A) | Appellate Division

## Proposition cited on the site

Leading authority for the **merchant/manufacturer-seller test** for consequential damages via the *actio empti*. Consequential damages (beyond refund / price reduction) for latent defects require an express warranty, fraud, or a "merchant/manufacturer seller" who publicly professes skill. This constrains when buyers can recover losses beyond the core aedilitian relief.
