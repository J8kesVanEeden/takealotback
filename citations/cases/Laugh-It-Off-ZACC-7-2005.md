# Laugh It Off Promotions CC v SAB International
## [2005] ZACC 7 | Constitutional Court | 27 May 2005 | Moseneke J

## Proposition cited on the site

Balancing of trade-mark dilution (Trade Marks Act s 34(1)(c) defence — non-commercial criticism/commentary) against freedom of expression under s 16 of the Constitution. The court held that a trade-mark proprietor seeking to block expressive use must prove **likely substantial economic detriment** — mere offence is insufficient.

## Why it matters to TakealotBack

This is the single most important SA constitutional-law anchor for a domain-name / trade-mark-using criticism site. It establishes the balancing test and places the evidentiary burden on the trade-mark proprietor, not the critic.
