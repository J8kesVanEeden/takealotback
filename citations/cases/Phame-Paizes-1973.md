# Phame (Pty) Ltd v Paizes
## 1973 (3) SA 397 (A) | Appellate Division

## Proposition — important accuracy note

Phame is the leading SA authority on **dicta et promissa** — the extension of aedilitian relief to innocent misrepresentations about the quality of the *merx*. It is NOT the source of the classical three-part latent-defect test (hidden / present at sale / material), which traces to *Dibley v Furter* 1951 (4) SA 73 (C) and *Glaston House v Inag* 1977 (2) SA 846 (A).

An earlier version of aedilitian-remedies.md cited Phame as the source of the three-part test; that was corrected on 2026-04-24 following the Claude Desktop copy-check audit.
