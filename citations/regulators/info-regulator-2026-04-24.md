# Information Regulator — POPIA complaint channels (verified 2026-04-24)

## Primary channel

- **eServices Portal:** `eservices.inforegulator.org.za` — primary channel for POPIA complaints since 2024. Since 7 April 2025, security-compromise (data breach) reporting through the portal is mandatory.

## Secondary channels

- POPIA complaints email (fallback): `POPIAComplaints@inforegulator.org.za`
- General enquiries email: `enquiries@inforegulator.org.za`
- Phone: `010 023 5200`

## Scope

The Information Regulator is the statutory body under POPIA and PAIA, with remedial powers including enforcement notices and fines. Complaints under POPIA (unlawful processing, excessive data collection, failure to secure) can be lodged free of charge.
