# Advertising Regulatory Board — current contact details

## Contact

- Website: `arb.org.za` (note: redirects to `www.arb.org.za`)
- Complaints email: `complaint@arb.org.za`

## Succession from ASA

The ARB succeeded the Advertising Standards Authority (ASA) following the ASA's liquidation in late September / October 2018. The exact commencement date varies slightly across secondary sources; "October 2018" is the generally-accepted start. The site uses "October 2018" which is within tolerance — not re-verified to a specific day in this snapshot.

## Scope of rulings

- ARB rulings bind ARB members.
- Non-members can still be the subject of a published ruling; publication creates reputational pressure but not binding compliance.
